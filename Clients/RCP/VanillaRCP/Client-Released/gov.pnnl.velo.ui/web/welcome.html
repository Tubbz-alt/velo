A customizable, reusable and collaborative knowledge management system based on commercial-grade, open-source technologies, is designed explicitly for managing scientific work over the entirety of a project's life cycle. Already deployed operationally in several U.S. government agencies, Velo is the product of more than a decade of development and expertise. The result is a scalable total data management platform that can be tailored and launched to sites within weeks.
