What is meant by “data that validates results”?

Here is the Department of Energy’s glossary description of “Validate”:

“In the context of this statement, validate means to support, corroborate, verify, or otherwise determine the legitimacy of the research findings. Validation of research findings could be accomplished by reproducing the original experiment or analyses; comparing and contrasting the results against those of a new experiment or analyses; or by some other means.” (http://science.energy.gov/funding-opportunities/digital-data-management/#Validate)
