Last Updated

This is the date of which the latest version of your resource was produced. The format for the date is as much of YYYY-MM-DDThh:mm:ss.sTZD as is relevant for the resource.

YYYY = 4-digit year
MM = 2-digit month
DD = 2-digit day
T  = literal "T" denotes the start of the time portion
hh = hour
mm = minutes
ss = seconds
s = fraction of a second
TZD = time zone

Example: 2015-08-15T12:34:56.01+07:00

  • “2015-08-15” = August 15, 2015
  • T = separator between date and time
  • 12:34:56.01 = 12:34 PM, and 56.01 seconds
  • +07:00 = Coordinated universal time (UTC) plus seven hours
