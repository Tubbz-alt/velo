Data Quality

Data quality is about describing the state of the data, quality control protocols applied to it, anomalies discovered, and other characteristics of it that would be important for a user to know. A data quality document should be uploaded in Velo to fulfill this field.

The depth of the topic of data quailty exceeds the scope of this help section. Here are several webpages for assistance with understanding data quality:

Environmental Protection Agency - Quality Management Tools - Data Quality Assessment

Health and Human Services - Information Quality Guidelines

National Science Foundation - Data Quality Standards

U.S. Geological Survey - Data Management: Manage Quality
