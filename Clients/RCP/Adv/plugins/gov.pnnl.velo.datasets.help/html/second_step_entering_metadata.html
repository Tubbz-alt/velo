Describe Data

Metadata are pieces of data about data. The purpose of metadata is to serve both human and machine-readable environments when describing all of the attributes of, in this case, your dataset. Filling-in the metadata fields in the Metadata Editor allows your data to be:

  • Compliant with requirements for making Federally funded research available to the public.
  • Clearly described on a landing page that the public will see.
  • More discoverable.
  • Easier for others to cite.
  • Provided with a Digital Object Identifier (DOI).

There are four sections of information you need to complete.

  1. Information About Your Dataset
  2. Data Quality & Methods
  3. Landing Page Customization
  4. Information for OSTI
