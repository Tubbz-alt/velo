Originating Research Organization

Use the spelled-out text exactly as shown in the Originating Research Organization Authority at https://www.osti.gov/elink/authorities.jsp. (Click the hyperlink and select Originating Research Organization from the drop down list.) If work for this product was done at more than one research organization, multiple values may be listed.

The primary DOE organization should be listed first, followed by any others. If non-DOE organizations are included, input the spelled-out, full name of the organization.

Example

One research organization

Not Acceptable

PNNL

Acceptable

Pacific Northwest National Laboratory (PNNL), Richland, WA (United States)

More than one research organization

Not Acceptable

PNNL; WSU

Acceptable

Pacific Northwest National Laboratory (PNNL), Richland, WA (United States); Washington State University, Pullman, WA (United States)
