Get a Draft DOI

This step is optional.

When the situation arises where you will be publishing a paper before the dataset is published, you will need a draft digital object identifier (DOI) to use in your paper to cite your dataset. The Department of Energy Office of Science and Technical Information (OSTI) allows you to receive a draft DOI.

All that is required to receive a draft DOI is a landing page for the dataset. Velo was developed so that a user can provide all of the information needed to create the landing page and make the DOI request to OSTI.

After your data has undergone appropriate reviews (step five), you will be presented with the mechanics to publish your dataset and make your draft DOI permanent (step six).
