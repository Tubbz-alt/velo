Prepare: Gather your data and information

The process of publishing your dataset starts off with gathering your data and the information about it. Here is a list of information to compile so you are prepared for the next step.

  • Name and email address for whomever wishes to respond to inquiries.
  • URL for where the dataset will be accessible.
  • Metadata schemas, taxonomies, data dictionaries, etc. that were used with this dataset.
  • A description about the dataset.
  • A title for the dataset.
  • Names of all authors associated with the dataset.
  • Date when the dataset will become accessible to outside users.
  • (If applicable) Date when the dataset was last updated.
  • Applicable licenses.
  • Knowledge about any access restrictions on your dataset.
  • Publications that cite your dataset.
  • Webpages or websites related to your dataset.
  • Keywords that would make your dataset more discoverable by users.
  • For spatial data, the geographic coordinates that apply to the dataset.
  • For temporal data, the start/end datetime.
  • (If available) Dataset product number.
  • Contract number(s) under which the dataset was created.
  • Names of sponsoring organizations.
