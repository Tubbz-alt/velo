ORCiD

An ORCiD is a digital identifier for a person who produces material - such as journal articles, datasets, conference papers, software, and more - in a field of research. It assists the research community with disambiguating names when citing material. A person does not need more than one ORCiD.

If an author has an ORCiD and wants to use it, they can enter it in this field.

If you are intersted in an ORCiD or want more information, visit orcid.org.
