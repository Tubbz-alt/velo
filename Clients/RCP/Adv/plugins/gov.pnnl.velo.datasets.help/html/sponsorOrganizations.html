Sponsoring Organization(s)

Select the DOE Program Office and sub-Program Office that funded the work described in the STI Product. Use the spelled-out text as shown in the Sponsoring Organization Authority at https://www.osti.gov/elink/authorities.jsp. (Click on this hyperlink and choose Sponsoring Organization from the drop-down menu.) If funding for this work was provided from more than one organization, multiple values may be listed.

The primary DOE sponsor should be listed first, followed by any others in descending order of dollar amount of funding. If any of the others are not included in the Sponsoring Organization Authority, include the spelled-out, full name of the other sponsoring organization(s).

Example

Not Acceptable

BER

Acceptable

USDOE Office of Science (SC), Biological and Environmental Research (BER) (SC-23)
